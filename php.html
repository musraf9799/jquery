Overflow Example

1. overflow: visible (default)

Yeh bahut lamba text hai jo box ke bahar nikal raha hai aur aap ise clearly dekh sakte ho.

2. overflow: hidden

Yeh bahut lamba text hai jo box ke bahar nikal raha hai lekin hidden hone ki wajah se cut ho jayega.

3. overflow: scroll

Yeh bahut lamba text hai jo box ke andar hai aur scroll bar hamesha dikhega.

4. overflow: auto

Yeh bahut lamba text hai aur jab zarurat hogi tabhi scroll bar dikhega.
